About Me

My name is Tanissha Gunjal, I am the developer of this app. I was inspired to create this app because of my hobby of playing the violin and I thought I would share this with everyone else, so I created Violin Pro. I wanted to help people read sheet music quickly. I started this app in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic.
